ANGELS OF GOD

    Return to Previous Page
Michael
Leader of the heavenly armies, protector of Israel and conqueror of evil.
Gabriel
Messenger of God, bearer of divine announcements such as the birth of Christ.
Raphael
Healer and guide, protector of travelers and bringer of divine remedies.
Uriel
Angel of wisdom and enlightenment, known for illuminating divine truth.
Selaphiel
Angel of prayer and worship who carries the faithful’s prayers to God.
Jegudiel
Protector of leaders, symbolizes divine reward for righteous labor.
Barachiel
Bringer of blessings and grace, often shown with roses.
Zadkiel
Angel of mercy, known for halting Abraham’s sacrifice.
Remiel
Angel of resurrection, hope, and judgment.
Raguel
Angel of justice and harmony, overseer of other angels.
Metatron
Heavenly scribe and closest to God’s throne.
Sandalphon
Twin of Metatron, angel of music and prayers.
Phanuel
Angel of repentance, one of the four who stand before God.
Sariel
Punisher of the fallen and guide of the souls.
Samael
Ambiguous angel of death and judgment, sometimes seen as an accuser or destroyer.
Israfel
Archangel of music and poetry, trumpet of Judgment.
Ariel
Angel of nature, protector of the Earth.
Cassiel
Angel of solitude and time.
Chamael
Angel of love, peace, and healing.
Zophiel
Angel of beauty and divine order.
Azrael
Angel of death and the afterlife.
Kerubiel
Mighty cherub, angelic guardian of the throne of God.
Seraphiel
Chief of the Seraphim, angel of purification and divine fire.
Duma
Angel of silence and stillness.
Jeremiel
Angel of visions and prophecy, who guides the souls.
